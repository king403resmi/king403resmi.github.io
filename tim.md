## Budaya Kerja

### Remote-first, result-oriented
Output diukur by deliverable — bukan jam duduk di kantor. Core hours overlap 10:00–15:00 WIB untuk meeting.

### Code review culture
Setiap PR butuh ≥1 approval. No merge to main without green CI.

### Blameless learning
Post-mortem setelah insiden — fokus sistem & proses, bukan individu.

### Growth mindset
Budget sertifikasi (AWS, GCP, security) dan 2 conference/tahun per engineer.

### Team gatherings
Quarterly meetup (Jakarta/Bali rotation) — team building + strategy alignment.
