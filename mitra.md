## Program Kemitraan B2B

### White-label Development
Agency atau consultancy yang butuh tim engineering extended — KING403 deliver under your brand (NDA + SLA).

### Referral Partner
Komisi 10% untuk referral klien yang sign contract (terms apply).

### Technology Partner
Vendor SaaS/infrastructure ingin integrasi ke platform klien KING403 — co-selling opportunity.

Hubungi: [partnership@king403.com](mailto:partnership@king403.com)
